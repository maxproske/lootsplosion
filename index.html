Brand Identity

About

Purchase loot boxes for all your favourite video games at our convenient, centralized hub, at the most attractive prices on the market.

Lootsplosion is intended to be a satirical website. Over the last few years, the gaming industry has seen a steady shift towards a new monetization strategy. Loot boxes (sometimes loot crate) is a consumable virtual item that can be redeemed for a randomised selection of further virtual items. Loot boxes lock desirable content behind these microtransactions with randomized payouts. Instead of “pay $X For Y item” the model is to purchase and open an unknown number of boxes until you get lucky and find something you wanted.

Inspiration

The phrase “Loot-splosion” was coined by the Borderlands video game franchise, that describes a rare and veritable explosion of guns, ammunition, money, and other loot, in reward for defeating a boss.

What actions the user can perform:

  1. Browse loot boxes for all games, with details page for each product.
  2. Filter results by game, price, and type (cosmetic, EXP boosts, etc.).
  3. Virtual shopping cart and checkout system.
  4. Connect Steam, Battle.net, Origin accounts to deposit purchases.
  5. Loyalty center to rewards buyers for repeat purchases and referrals.

Personas

Persona #1: Andrew

Andrew is a 32-year-old hardcore gamer. His favourite games are Halo 5: Guardians and Desinty 2. Unfortunately, he works full-time as a graphic designer, and doesn't have the time to play enough to progress through these games normally. Andrew wants to pay in order to stay competitive in the online multiplayer scene.

Persona #2: Laura

Laura is a 23-year-old midcore gamer. She spends time everyday playing Multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games, such as League of Legends and Dota 2. Laura is a completionist, and must collect all the "skins" or varients of her favourite champions. But it would take forever to collect them all through normal play.
